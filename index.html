Moons of Mars

Phobos

Phobos, Mars’s larger moon, is a small, irregularly shaped object with a mean radius of 11 km and is seven times more massive than the outer moon, Deimos.

Discovered by
Asaph Hall
Discovery date
August 17, 1877
Adjectives
Phobian
